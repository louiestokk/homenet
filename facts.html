What are the rules about owning land or houses in Zanzibar as a foreigner?

Through ZIPA, which is the department for leasing houses or land in Zanzibar, foreigners can lease land or houses for up to 99 years. Foreigners have the right to start a business in Zanzibar and the company itself can own a house or land directly in Zanzibar. So these 2 options are what apply to foreign. We at Zanzihome.com can help you with the whole process, which can take between 1-3 weeks.
